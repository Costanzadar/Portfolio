Quantum Card Deck

A quantum card deck was developed through research and brainstorming sessions during the period of collaboration with Deborah Nas at TU Delft and Quantum Delta NL, aimed at facilitating engaging discussions on the future applications of quantum computing during workshops and conventions.

The problem framing centers on proactively addressing the ethical and social implications of quantum computing by anticipating potential challenges, collaborating with experts to evaluate feasible solutions, and aligning the technology's applications with sustainable development goals and social values.

ACTIONS – Use active verbs to motivate users to explore and apply the technology effectively.

VALUES – Integrate principles like sustainability, inclusivity, and fairness to ensure a positive societal impact.

APPLICATIONS – Quantum computing spans computing, sensing, and communication, each offering transformative potential.

The group starts by creating scenarios with the three initial cards (value, action, and application) and adds more as needed, using the A4 sheet for additional context, resulting in a collection of ideas that merges value and real-world application.
